About

I am a third-year PhD student in Computer Science at Simon Fraser University (SFU). My research centers on non-stationary environments and sequential decision-making, with a focus on continual learning and reinforcement learning. I develop methods that preserve plasticity while enabling robust knowledge transfer, and I’m experienced with PyTorch-based deep learning, large-scale simulation (Isaac Sim/Lab), and ROS2-enabled robotics.
